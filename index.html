What is Salesforse?

Salesforce is the world’s no.1 Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform developed by Salesforce.com and it’s the first one connecting CRM with cloud concept. It provides businesses to understand and improve relationships with their respective customers.

cloud

Salesforce Developer

A Salesforce developer is an individual who has the abilities and knowledge of an Admin and beyond that they have coding capabilities to develop cloud based applications through the force.com platform with an in-depth understanding of Salesforce and its limitations. A Salesforce developer must possess knowledge of VisualForce and Salesforce Lightning development with experience in coding using APEX.
